Demo Applicattion:
Unzip StickperPapers.zip to 
XJGSDMFaceDemo-win32\StickperPapers
XJGSDMFaceDemo-win64\StickperPapers
,then the corresponding application can run.


SDK:
Unzip StickperPapers.zip to 
XJGSDMFaceSDKProjectDemo-win32\StickperPapers
XJGSDMFaceSDKProjectDemo-win64\StickperPapers
,then the corresponding SDK can use sticker papers.

XJGSDMFaceDemo-win32\StickperPapers
XJGSDMFaceDemo-win64\StickperPapers
